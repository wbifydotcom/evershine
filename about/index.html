About Us

Knowledge is power, Information is liberating.

Evershine college is a nationally recognised educational institute that offers courses related to Aged care, Disability and Community Services. We provide intensive support to our students to gratify their learning needs. In terms of flexibility, our online sessions for "Certificate III in individual support" will be conducted in accordance with student's suitability. So, our study pattern revolves around your life. Our highly qualified trainers are committed to provide best educational opportunities.

We ensure that all students are well supported to achieve academic success. We are passionate to help students to become successful professionals and make an impact on the world. Course Accessibility

We are offering Australia’s leading Aged Care Course “Certificate III in Individual Support”. This course is designed in a way that can fit in every student’s lifestyle and you can complete your qualification online from anywhere in Australia. If you are passionate about working in health industry and planning for amazing job opportunities in nursing profession. Then, this course will help you to achieve your desired goals.

Training Needs

Evershine College is committed to provide high quality training material and resources to facilitate your learning needs. Our motivated trainers will help you to gain essential knowledge and skills to provide quality care for elderly’s. We ensure that our students are well supported during their study period, we offer study material online as well as printed copies, if needed.

Vocational Placement

We have professional links with the aged care facilities and regular engagement with in the community that helps our students to gain essential workplace skills. Therefore, you can rely on our organisation to achieve practical knowledge that fulfil your employer’s demands.

Online Support

Your trainer is just one call away. We provide extensive support to our students all over the Australia. Students can call or email their trainer, if they have any queries related to their course. We are committed to provide best quality study material and resources to keep your knowledge up to date as per the course requirement.
