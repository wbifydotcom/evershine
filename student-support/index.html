Student Support

Right from the enrollment for aged care and disability course to getting placed, Evershine College stands by its students to help them learn better and prosper. At Star, you get access to round-the-clock study support via trainers, counseling sessions aimed at making you more career-focused, student service help desk, a library stocked with books and journals you'd need for your course and much more.

Student services help desk

In case any student has issues with studies, administrative concerns or simply wishes to talk about the course and/or career, we have a special help desk for the purpose. Here, general queries are answered, information about the course is provided, access to study material is given and counseling services are set up, if needed.

Trainers are just a call away

Trainers and assessors of Evershine College are within your easy reach, whenever you wish to discuss anything related to studies. Their contact details, like Email IDs and telephone numbers are given to all students in the subject outline and are also available online.

Encouragement to form study groups

Study groups are an effective way to study together and receive feedback of the fellow students in order to understand and learn better. We continuously encourage our students to form study groups and study together. We only organize study groups and leave students to study freely and independently in a friendly environment.

Students with special needs

Evershine College provides students with special needs the same supportive and receptive environment as to other students. Here, students with special needs get extra and specialized academic support from the trainers, along with equal opportunities to access facilities and participate in courses.

Library and media services

Evershine College houses an exhaustive range of books and other media resources related to courses we provide. Students can borrow books, DVDs and also the scanned copies of journal articles and book chapters related to the course. A browsing room is also available wherein internet facility is provided.
